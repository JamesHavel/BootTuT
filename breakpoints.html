Breakpoints

Breakpoints are the sizes where you screen will change layouts. As you can see in the Rows section, at certain screen sizes the content will change to a different layout. With Bootstrap, this concept is made a lot more simple if the content is put into a column and container.

This one has one breakpoint

Just 

col-md-4
being the only one. So once this breakpoint is hit, all the content wrapped in the 
class="container"
the 
 class="row"
and the 
class="col-md-4"
it will turn itself into one column.
